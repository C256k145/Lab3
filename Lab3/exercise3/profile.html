The Rarest Pepe

The Rare Pepe, sometimes known as just pepe, is of mysterious origins, though there is speculation that he is originally from the amazon, and lost his home due to deforestation. As the name describes, a sighting is a rare occurrence-he does not show himself in public willingly, his sightings are typically the result of local children poking fun. With a most dismal expression, he is most often in hiding. Rumor has it that he spends his days in mourning of his lost home, searching endlessly for a spot to begin anew.
